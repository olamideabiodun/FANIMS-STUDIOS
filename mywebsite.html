Get to know me better

A 19 years old illustrator from Nigeria

From my teens, I used to make absolutely everything into miniature books, experimenting in various materials, most often gouache, acrylic, colored pencil and collage to create warm and friendly illustrations. And in recent years I have used Illustrator more and more, and now my illustrations are almost entirely digital. I have illustrated for picture books, children's magazines, wedding invitations, logo design, stickers, website designs, and hand-lettered typography.

I believe that talent is a myth that can definitely stop us from achieving our creative goals. I believe that practice makes us perfect and that perfection is nothing but an imagination. Likewise, I believe in community and collaboration over competition, which is why I help people to run their businesses at their best. I used to blend numerous freelance projects with my in-house jobs during the last few years, and now my time is entirely devoted to freelance tasks.
